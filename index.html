About Me

I was born and raised in a small town right next to Indianapolis. While I am currently a web developer, I originally started my professional career as a band teacher. While I still have a deep love for both music and teaching, web development is a career that I both excel at and enjoy.

I recived my education through a web development bootcamp at the Eleven Fifty Academy in Indianapolis, IN. Combined with my Bachelors of Music Education from Ball State University, I was able to gain both the hard skills for coding as well as being able to communicate with, relate to, and serve my clients as well as my community.
